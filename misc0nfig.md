# **misc0nfig 2025 attendee announcement**

## Only three days left!
</center>
Hi! First of all, I want to thank you for your interest and support in Warwick Cyber Security Society's first ever conference: misc0nfig 2025! We're very excited to be bringing it to you!

---
## Schedule
We have announced our final schedule on [our society website](https://warwickcybersoc.com/misc0nfig). This will be updated to reflect any last-minute changes to the schedule, but a paper copy will also be provided on the day.

---
## Key details
- Arrival will be FROM 10:00, with the welcome talk starting at 10:45.
- Lunch will be between 13:00 and 14:00 in Recharge.
- The building must be vacated by 17:30.

---
## Location
The event will be located in the **[International Manufacturing Centre](https://maps.app.goo.gl/4PpGcfhSpC6YNRJJ8)** at the University of Warwick.

---
## Transport
For parking, please use [Car Park 15](https://maps.app.goo.gl/1AXvvz8eTDkFpgqa9) or the [Lynchgate Car Park](https://maps.app.goo.gl/ev4s1BfnTp3j8qmM7). This should help to minimise congestion at the smaller car parks on campus.
You can access information about parking fees and more information on the [University of Warwick Car Parking site](https://warwick.ac.uk/about/campus-journey/car-parking/general_parking/).


If you are travelling by train, you can travel to Coventry railway station, and get the 11 or the 12X bus from the [bus stop just outside the station](https://maps.app.goo.gl/61Lxpk5wuyR8nQ8L7). If you wish, you can also travel to Canley railway station, which is within walking distance of campus.


If you are travelling by bus, you will need to get off at the [University Interchange](https://maps.app.goo.gl/3VZcCQHnGGjzEK917), where you can walk to the event.
