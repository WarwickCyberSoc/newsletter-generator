## ❓ What is a CTF?
- A **Capture The Flag** competition is a hacking contest with lots of hacking
challenges in several different categories
- You compete as **part of a team** to get as many secret strings (or "**flags**")
as you can from the challenges

## ❓ What is IntakeCTF?
IntakeCTF is a **CTF designed for freshers**! No experience needed, we've spent 
our summer developing **beginner-friendly challenges** to introduce you to
competing in CTFs!
