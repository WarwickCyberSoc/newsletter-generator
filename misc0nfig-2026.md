# misc0nfig 2026: final details and reminder

Hi everyone! This is your reminder email about **Warwick Cyber Security Society's misc0nfig conference** tomorrow! Run
in collaboration with **CyberWomen@Warwick**!

Feel free to check out the **schedule** before you arrive, or you can make decisions on the day: [warwickcybersoc.com/misc0nfig](https://warwickcybersoc.com/misc0nfig)

---

## Arriving at the event
- Doors will be open from **10:00**. 
- You will be required to leave the venue **shortly after 18:00**
- You are welcome to bob in and out, though you will need to be **wearing your conference lanyard to get back in** (this will be provided when you sign-in).
- The event will be taking place in the **International Manufacturing Centre**, at the **University of Warwick**. There is parking available, but the university campus is also accessible via train (Canley station is nearby) and bus (12X from Coventry, U1/U2 from Leamington Spa).
- **Car Park 15** and **Car Park 8** are close to the building and should have space.

---
## Interrupt Labs: IoT Vulnerability Workshop
- There is **limited capacity** for this session, and it will be allocated on a **first-come, first-served** basis.
- If the session is full, you will be asked to attend a different session.
- If you wish to use your own device (and you're more than welcome to!) you will need to set up some basic software beforehand. You can access the tutorial document **[here](https://drive.google.com/file/d/1GR-R-F4NflC8v_ijj9g7TJZ2_BvWDp35/view?usp=sharing)**.
- However, we will have desktops with the tools already set up if you wish to use those.

--- 
## Key Reminders
- Please **ensure you have the correct ticket**! Sixth-form and college students are entitled to Pre-Uni Student tickets, but other attendees must book the Attendee ticket.
- If you have booked a Pre-Uni Student ticket, please **ensure you bring ID** (ideally government ID, but student ID will do if not).
- Event **staff will be wearing black lanyards**, and potentially Warwick Cyber Security Society hoodies. Please talk to them if you have any problems and/or comments.
- **Lunch** will be provided **shortly after 13:00** (1pm). You do not need to bring food, or need to buy food, though you are welcome to should you wish.
- **Refreshments** will be available for the duration of the conference.
