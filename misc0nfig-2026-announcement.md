# misc0nfig 2026
**Tickets are now available for the annual CyberSoc Conference!**

**Get yours now** on the **[Warwick SU Website](https://www.warwicksu.com/events/61481/28067/)**!

**Check out** the **[conference timetable on our website](https://warwickcybersoc.com/misc0nfig)**!

It's that time once again folks! misc0nfig is back and bigger than ever! 
We've got:

- **Amazing Talks** on everything from **car hacking** to **static code 
analysis**!
- A **camera hacking workshop**!
- A **networking area** with **recruiters**!
- **Free food** and **drink**!
- A **CTF with prizes**!
- And the opportunity to **meet other students** at the **biggest student-run
security conference in England**!

We're honoured to be sponsored this year by **Bending Spoons**, **CrowdStrike**,
**Interrupt Labs**, **Reversec**, **JUMPSEC** and **Conscia**! More on those
lovely folks later :)
