## 📖  Constitution and Amendments
The **[CyberSoc Constitution](https://docs.google.com/document/d/1dPxwFj8ugNhZBSv8tgiGRyC_wVWTfMEMszRGTkFwvHE/edit?usp=sharing)** details: 
- **how elections work**
- **what the society does**
- **what each role's responsibilities are**.

You can **submit an amendment** (a suggested change) to the constitution. This could be e.g.:
- A **new role**
- Making **changes to a current role**

### ❓ How to Submit an Amendment
- Download the constitution, and **make any changes in red text**. **Strikethrough** any sections you're proposing to **remove**.
- Submit your edited Constitution through **[this Google Form](https://forms.gle/e8EKdPLUZ8JSe4Hp6)**
- By submitting your amendment, you agree to be **named as its proposer**.
- **Come to the AGM** to defend/discuss your amendment.

### The AGM
- 📆When:  **05-03-2026 19:00-21:00**
- 📍 Where: S0.11
- At the AGM we'll **vote on candidates and amendments**
- We need **at least 7% of society members to be present**
- The **returning officer** is **Peter Walker**. He'll be running the election, and **must remain impartial to all candidates and proposals**
