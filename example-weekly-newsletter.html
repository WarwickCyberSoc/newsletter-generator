Events This Week

⚙️ IntroToCTF: What Can I do With CTF?

📅 When: Monday (2nd March) 18:00-20:00

📍 Where: IMC 2.46

Last IntroToCTF session before the election folks! A few third years are gonna speak briefly about their technical security grad roles, and how CTF challenges helped them get to where they are. We've got folks from:

  • Vulnerability Research
  • Penetration Testing
  • Digital Forensics
  • and more!

This is a more laid-back session, and a good opportunity to ask some questions. I'd recommend bringing your CV, there'll probably be time for the speakers to review it for you.

🔊 🍕 Talk Series: The Subaru incident or: how I learned to stop worrying and love the CAN bus (feat Jamie and FREE PIZZA )

📅 When: Wednesday (4th March) 14:00-15:00

📍 Where: IMC 0.02

👤 Who: Jamie Madden

Our wonderful social sec Jamie is giving an introduction into the world of automotive cyber security, discussing case studies ranging from unlocking doors using the CAN bus to gaining full domain admin of corporate systems with a car as the attack vector!

We couldn't let such a cool talk pass us by without some free pizza, so come help us eat it!

🍕 CyberSoc AGM and Elections With FREE PIZZA

📅 When: Thursday (5th March) 19:00-21:00

📍 Where: S 0.18

It's time for CyberSoc's annual general meeting. Like all societies we have an AGM to discuss what we've done this year, and vote on constitutional amendments.

More importantly though, you'll be voting for the new exec! Every candidate for every role will give a short speech and you'll get the chance to ask them questions. Listening to all those speeches is hungry work so we're ordering pizza!

⭕ 🍻 Circle: Dress as the Exec

📅 When: Saturday (7th March) 19:30-late

📍 Where: Kelsey's in Leamington Spa

It's the last circle of the current exec! In honour of the old farts who are retiring after this week, we're poking fun one last time. Please come dressed as a member of the current exec! Will you put on stilts to dress as Artem or Sam? Let your hair down as Jamie or Ellie? Don an old man jumper to dress as the devilishly handsome, incredibly stylish, monumentally humble VP?

Come join us as we celebrate "out with the old, in with the new" by getting drunk with a load of doppelgangers :)
